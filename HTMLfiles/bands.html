Ivuga Band

IvugaLive is a live entertainment band that offers services for private and public functions. It was born from the great musical passion and skill and is dedicated to giving the Kampala entertainment scene a touch of musical genius.

@ivugaBand
Magic Hornz Band

Magic Hornz performances combine Jazz, traditional African Beats, folk, Raggae and Salsa with an original mix of traditional and contemporary sounds from around globe, giving the music a punchy front line-up to flavour the performance with magic of the Horns.

@BandHornz
Canaan Gents Band

Canaan Gents is an Acapella vocal band devoted to making Christ famous while pursuing excellence and setting a trend in Capella music. The band sings in stunning vocal harmony without instrumental accompaniment. It's an engaging mix of tightly-crafted music. Canaan gents has stamped its mark in the musical arena by releasing an EP dubbed "Nkwagala EP" of 8 songs as well Uganda’s first A cappella music video for their song “Sing”.

@Canaangents
The New Eagles Band

The New Eagles band has won not only the admiration of musical pundits, critics and local audiences, but also the support of distinguished international music promoters. This band was founded by the singing couple Geofrey Lutaaya and Irene Namatovu.

De New Eagles
Janzi Band

Janzi is a Ugandan afro-fusion, contemporary and world music band formed on 27 August 2009 by Ssewa Ssewa and friends Abraham Sekasi, Jackson Kirya and Steven Oundo. The band has a hybrid repertoire of music, including notable covers as well as an original ethno-pop discography.

@JanziBand
Afrigo Band

The Afrigo Band is a musical band in Uganda. It is the longest-lasting musical group in the history of Uganda, having existed for 46 years by August 2021. The band is famous for alot of its works like the famous, "Sikulimba" .

@Afrigoband256
Golden Band

The golden band was founded after the falling out of the old Eagles Production band. The Golden band has many popular artists such as Mesearch Ssemakula and Catherine Kusasira.

The Golden Band

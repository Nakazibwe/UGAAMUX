About Us

UGAAMUX is a platform that registers the creatives in Uganda, and gives artistes a uniform platform to showcase their work to their fans. Fans can view the works of celebrities, and creatives on the other hand, have a platform to show the public their authentic work, their fans probably didnot about. Get to know your celebrities more and their work. As a creative get in touch with UGAAMUX to showcase your work to the public. Lets re-introduce you to the Ugandan talent because, Ugandan talent lives here.

Ugandan Talent Lives Here!!!
History

Formed from the need to build an Archive for all creatives in Uganda, an intiative by National Cultural Center in conjuction with Uganda Performing Arts Association of Uganda

Mission

To give creatives a platform to showcase talent.

Vision

To have the biggest and well-rounded database for all creatives in Uganda.

Goal

Our goal is to give all creatives an equal platform.

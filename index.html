Afrigo Band

The Afrigo Band is a musical band in Uganda. It is the longest-lasting musical group in the history of Uganda, having existed for 46 years by August 2021.The band was formed by a group of eight musicians led by their band leader, vocalist, and alto saxophonist, Moses Matovu, who continued to lead the band on its 38th anniversary. The band plays to their fans in Uganda and regularly tours in Europe and the United States to play to Ugandans in the Diaspora.

@Afrigoband256

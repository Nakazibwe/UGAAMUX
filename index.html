Azawi

Jose Chameleone

Chameleon started his music career in the early 1990s with the Ogopa Deejays, a Kenyan record label then and managed to gain popularity because of his talent across the entire African continent. He has 13 known albums and several songs like the popular "Wale Wale". He is the founder of Leone Island label which he uses to record his music.

@JChameleone

Fik Fameica

Fik Fameica fame came in 2015 with "Pistol" under the Black Man Town music label.He started a recording label called Fresh Gang Records with his international manager known as Skizzie, Wembley Mo, Mozelo Kidz, Sam Kikumba, Promoter Brian & DJ Ranks Showmaster. Fik has nine albums, and several songs like the popular "Omu Bwati".

@FFameica

Juliana Kanyomozi

Juliana went solo with her music career, and evolved from Karaoke singer, Kanyomozi has risen to become one of the biggest divas in Ugandan music and East Africa at large. She has 14 known albums and several songs including the popular, "Kanyimbe"

@JKanyomozi

MUN*G

MUN*G is a rap artist under the label BABOON FOREST ENTERMAINMENT.He has been in the rap game for some time, to many he came to be known after he released songs like “Oh my Goodness” and “Kyaba Too God. He has 19 albums including the famous,"Kyaba Too Much”

@MunGmato

Vinka

Late 2016, the record label she worked for asked her to convert into a musician. After considerations, Vinka took the opportunity and signed a record deal under stage name Vinka in Febuary of 2017. She is currently signed to the Swangz Avenue label and she has 17 albums, this includes the famous, "Love Panic".

@IamVinka

Sheeba Karungi

Sheeba is a musician, dancer and actress who debuted her acting career in Queen of Katwe as Shakira. After quitting Obsessions, a dance group she joined in 2006, she rose to recognition upon the release of her hit single "Ice Cream".She is currently has 8 albums and is known for her popular works like, "Twesana". .

@Ksheebah1

Maurice Kirya

@mauricekirya

Rema Namakula

Rema Namakula, popularly referred to as Rema is a musical artist based in Uganda. She started her professional music career under a fellow artist BebeCool who mentored her and she later went solo. She has 6 known albums and is famous for most of her popular works like, "Oliwange" .

@REMAUG1

Eddy Kenzo

Eddy Kenzo, is a Ugandan singer and music executive who is a founder and a member of Big Talent Entertainment. He received international attention after the release of his 2014 single, "Sitya Loss". In total, he has released 8 albums, including Roots in 2018 and most recently Made in Africa in 2021.

@eddykenzoficial

Bebe Cool

Bebe Cool is a ragga musician from Uganda. He started his career around 1997 in Nairobi, Kenya, he was one of the first artists affiliated with Ogopa DJs, a production house and record label in Kenya. He has 8 albums and has mentored many artists through his record label Gagamel Records.

@BebeCoolUG

Winnie Nwagi

Nwagi's music career began when she became the second runner-up during the second season of "CocaCola Rated Next" music competitions in 2014. She was signed by Swangz Avenue label and released a number of songs including Embeera, Katono Katono, Gwenoonya and Kyowulila. She released her commercially successful single Musawo in early 2016. She has 9 known albums.

@WinnieNwagi

Nina Rose

Nina Rose is a song writer, singer and model who has traversed the Ugandan entertainment industry with her exceptional talent. She has 2 known albums and is famous for her "Andeleya" hit that was a dual song with husband Andre.

@NinarozMusic

Navio

Navio is a musician and record producer. Starting off his career, he was part of the award-winning Hip hop group Klear Kut that introduced the term "Ugaflow" to describe Uganda's hip hop scene. Navio is best known for his successful solo career with hits such as 'Ngalo', 'Bugumu', "One & Only", and "On and On" (with American R&B legend Keith Sweat)and has 7 known albums.

@naviomusic

Apass

Ugandan musician and songwriter,Apass has collaborated with various musicians including, A.Y. (musician), Goodlyfe from Uganda and Cinderella Sanyu among others. A Pass has written songs for Bebe Cool[7] and co-written 'Love I fell' with Michael Ross.[8] He has is famous for alot of his work like released a single Wuuyo the official soundtrack to Uganda movie Bala Bala Sese written by Usama Mukwaya. He has 6 known albums.

@IamApass

Spice Diana

Spice Diana, is a female Ugandan musician who started her music career early while studying at Makerere University. She has had several singles including 'Anti Kale', 'Bukete' and 'Bimpe'. Spice Diana has also collaborated with both local and international artists that include Pallaso, Ray G, Aganaga and Jamaican Orisha Sound among others.She has 4 known albums.

@SpiceDianaUg

Pia Pounds

Pia Pounds career started in 2011 when she released her first single Just the way you are featuring Ragga Dee. She was signed by Big Talent Entertainment label in 2018.Under Big Talent, Pounds' biggest releases were Tubawe and Wawangula. She later exited Big Talent Entertainment and started releasing her music as an independent artist in 2019. She then released songs like Byompa featuring Fik Fameica, Easy, and Delicious. She is mostly known for her recent hit , 'Tupaate'.

@pia_pounds

Naava Grey

Grey Naava is a Ugandan artist signed to Fenon Records.With R&B steadily becoming one of the sought after genres in the Ugandan music industry, one artist to watch out for is Grey Naava. After her first single “Aliba wani” received wide across the region, Naava’s management Fenon Records decided to release the second song “Nteredde”. And this sparked off what for very many years has been a successful career. She has 8 known albums.

@Iam_naavagrey

Daddy Andre

Daddy Andre is a Ugandan music producer, singer and songwriter who is one of the best music producers in the country and has now also ventured into singing as a career. He has 5 known albums of his own but he is mostly famous for his excellent producer skills as he is the brain behind most of the beats on the famous songs Ugandans know and love.

@daddyandreug

Lydia Jazmine

Music group Radio and Weasel signed Jazmine to a contract as a back-up singer at the start of her career. She is the back-up vocalist on the songs 'Ntunga' and 'Breath Away'. She later performed background vocal lyrics for Bebe Cool and Sheebah Karungi. Her first single was a duet with Rabadaba called "You Know", released in 2014. She has 17 albums that are publically known. And is famous for her works like, 'Same Way'.

@LydiahJazmine
